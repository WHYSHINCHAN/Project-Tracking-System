About Us

Created by students, for students

Project Hub was developed as a college project to address the challenges students face when managing their academic assignments, research papers, and group projects. Our goal is to provide a simple yet powerful tool that helps students stay organized and motivated.

Built using Python, Flask, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and MySQL, this application represents both our technical skills and our understanding of student needs. We welcome feedback and continuously work to improve the platform.
